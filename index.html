How to Play Word Detective

Welcome to Word Detective! Here's how to play:

  1. Guess the secret 5-letter word in 5 tries.
  2. Each guess must be a valid 5-letter word.
  3. After each guess, the color of the tiles will change to show how close your guess was to the word.

Color Meanings:

  • Green: Letter is correct and in the right spot.
  • Yellow: Letter is in the word but in the wrong spot.
  • Gray: Letter is not in the word.

Use the clues provided to help you guess the word. You can reveal up to 3 clues during the game.

Game Concept

In Word Detective, you'll be guessing a five-letter word that has multiple meanings or uses. This twist makes the game both challenging and educational, especially for ESL learners.

Good luck and have fun!
